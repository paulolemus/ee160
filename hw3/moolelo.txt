
I ka wa kahiko, ua noh kekahi keiki kane, `o Akkaka, me kona kupunahine
ma Honomu i ka mokupuni `o Hawai`i. Pa`ani mau `o Akaka me `elua 
kaikamahines i kela me keia la.  I kekahi ahiahi, ua holoi ke kupunahine
i kona malo, a kaul`ai `o ia i ka malo ma luna o ke ahi. I ke kakahiaka
a`e, ua komo `o Akaka i knoa malo a hele akula `o ia i waho e pa`ani me
na kaikamahine. Aue no ho`i e! Hoo`henehene nui laua ia ia no ka mea, 
hohono the malo i ka uahi.  Aloha `ino! (bummers) Nui loa kona hilahila,
a ho`i awiwi akula `o ia i ka hale a ue i kona kupunahine. Ma hope iho, pii`
`o ia me kana `ilio ma luna o ka wailele kokoke i ka hale, a lele ihola
laua.  Ua make loa laua a lilo i mau pohaku ma lalo pono o ka wailele.
He pohakku nui ke keiki kane, a he pohaku li`ili`i kana `ilio. Kaumaha
loa ke kupunahine, a lilo no ho`i `o ia i pohaku nui, ma luna a`e o ka
wailele.  Aia keia mau pohaku `ekolu ma laila i keia manawa.  Ua kapa
`ia kel wailele "`o Akaka" in keia manawa.
